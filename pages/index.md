### About Me

I am Adora Wang, a Ph.D. student from McGill University. I joined the McGill Integrated Program in Neuroscience (IPN) graduate program & Brain Repair and Integrative Neuroscience (BRaIN) program in Jan. 2024 under Prof. David Stellwagen's supervision. Before that, I gained research training from the Lab of Neurodegeneration (LND) at the University of Hong Kong and received a Master of Philosophy degree in neuroscience under Prof. Gordon Wong and Prof. Raymond Chang's supervision.
Feel free to contact me at jiaqiwangclinic@gmail.com
